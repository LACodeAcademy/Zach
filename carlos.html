Carlos Rodrigues

Carlos Rodrigues, aka Silver Fox, is the Senpai of the Seattle Ninjas. Through his guidance and teachings he has molded an elite ninja killing crew. Carlos, a master ninja himself, is the backbone for his assassins. His master mind works out all the details required before The Ninjas embark on their assassination missions.
